FAMILY DINNER

Craft A Meal For Everyone

What is Family Dinner?

Family Dinner is not a meal planning tool, but more than an RSVP.

It is a demo site built by a home chef, for home chefs.

Including you.

HOW IT WORKS:


First, dinner guests give their taste preferences

We are looking out for your budget and the environment, that's why we preload options that are local and in season

Then, the Family Dinner algorithm crunches the data

Taking into account your budget, guests, and time

And produces for you-the chef-the golden ticket!

A personalized plan for you to craft a dinner everyone will enjoy.
